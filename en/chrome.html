Chrome extension

Use your Chromebook or the Google Chrome browser extension to work with AAC communication boards on your computer. Customize the number and placement of buttons, as well as the font size, to suit your needs. Choose a background color or image for the buttons. Voice recording is also available. Open Board Format allows sharing with other applications.
