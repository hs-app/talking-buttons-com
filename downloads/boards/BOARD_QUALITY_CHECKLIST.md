# AAC Board Quality Checklist (SLP + AT)

## Core criteria
- Coverage includes: request, refuse, comment, social close, repair/help.
- Labels are short, high-frequency, and concrete.
- Large tap targets and predictable zones.
- High contrast and consistent symbol style.
- Import/export works as OBF/OBZ between Android and Chrome.

## Required cells (minimum)
- Yes, No, Help, Wait, Repeat, I don't understand.
- Pain/discomfort and basic needs (food, drink, toilet, rest).

## Metadata convention
- File name: `kebab-case-vMAJOR.MINOR.obf` (or `.obz`).
- Include title, locale, version, author, and purpose.
